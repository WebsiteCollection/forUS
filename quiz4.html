This quiz checks your understanding of the fifth and sixth period of U.S. history, from 1848 to 1898. It begins with the end of the Mexican-American War and goes all the way up to the Spanish-American War. Important concepts include the Civil War, Reconstruction, and Imperialism. Good Luck!
