About this Quiz

This quiz checks your understanding of the last two periods of U.S. history, from 1945 to the present. It begins with the aftermath of World War II up until current times. Important concepts include the Cold War, the Vietnam War, the Civil Rights Movement, the rise of conservatism and the War on Terrorism. Good Luck!
