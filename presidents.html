Presidents

We can’t talk about U.S. History without mentioning the people that are basically the face of the nation. That’s right, we’re talking about presidents.

The president is part of the Executive Branch of government and serve as the Chief Executive and Commander in Chief of the nation (basically he/she is like the CEO and commander of the military). Presidents have a two term limit with each term being four years, but there are exceptions (ex: Franklin D. Roosevelt).

As of now, we have had 45 presidents in all of U.S. History and here’s a brief breakdown of what each of them is known for.
